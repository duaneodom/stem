## Materials

1. glass container
2. balloon (pre-stretched)
3. vinegar
4. baking soda

## Description

Put vinegar in glass container (maybe 1 inch deep).  Put a tbsp or so of baking soda into the balloon.
Stretch balloon around the neck of the container careful not to spill any baking soda into container.
When ready tilt the balloon up to dump the baking soda into the container and watch the balloon inflate.


## Talking Points

- remember the carbon dioxide gas that was pressed into the drink at the factory
- we can make our own CO2
- the carbon dioxide was force into the drink mechanically. we will make a chemical reaction.
- vinegar is acetic acid. used in salad dressing, pickles and household cleaners.  CH3COOH
- baking soda is sodium bicarbonate. used in baking. NaHCO3
- baking soda makes baked goods rise by reacting with the acidic ingredients to make CO2 which forms little bubbles
